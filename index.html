Instructions:

To play the game, you first start your car by pressing the "enter" key. Now that the engine is running, you press the "right arrow" key to advance your car to the stagging area but, don't do over the line or you will foul and loose the race.

Once your car is in the stagging area, the "Christmas tree" lights will start the race's countdown. There are 3 "get ready" lights. The fourth is "GO!"

If you pass the start line before the "GO!" lights illuminate, you will foul and the 5th set of lights will show red indicating you lost the race.

On your mark, get set, good luck racing!

To start another race, press the "r" key or click the "Restart" button.
